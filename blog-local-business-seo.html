Local SEO Tips For Small Businesses

Local SEO helps your business appear when people search for services near them.

For example:

  • Website designer near me
  • Electrician in London
  • Plumber in Birmingham
  • Tutor in Manchester

To improve local rankings:

  • Create a Google Business Profile
  • Collect customer reviews
  • Add location pages
  • Use local keywords
  • Optimise your website speed

Businesses that invest in local SEO often receive more enquiries and more website traffic.
