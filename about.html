About Ready Set Site

Ready Set Site helps startups, trades and small businesses get online with clean, modern and affordable websites.

We focus on simple design, clear wording, mobile-friendly layouts and SEO-ready pages so your business looks professional and gets more enquiries.

Built for Small Businesses

Many small businesses need a professional website but do not want to pay large agency prices. Ready Set Site was created to make website design simple, affordable and easy to understand.

Whether you are launching a new business, updating an old website or trying to get more enquiries, we help you create a strong online presence without overcomplicating the process.

Clear Design

Your website should be easy to understand. We create clean layouts that help visitors quickly see what you offer and how to contact you.

SEO Friendly

Every website includes SEO-friendly structure, proper headings, page titles, descriptions and content that helps Google understand your business.

Mobile Ready

Most visitors use their phone. Your website will look good on mobiles, tablets and desktops, giving every visitor a smooth experience.

Our Website Process

We keep the process simple from start to finish. You tell us about your business, we plan the website structure, create the design, build the pages and prepare everything for launch.

  • We plan your website pages
  • We design a modern layout

  • We optimise for mobile and SEO
  • We help you launch online
