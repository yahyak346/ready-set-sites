How SEO Helps Your Website Get Found on Google

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation. It helps your website appear when customers search for your services on Google.

Without SEO, your website may look good but still struggle to get visitors. A properly optimised website gives your business a better chance of appearing in search results.

Good SEO includes:

  • Fast loading speed
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Clear page titles
  • Strong keywords
  • Proper headings and structure
  • Good written content

Local SEO is especially important for small businesses. It helps your business appear when people search for services near them.

At Ready Set Site, every website is built with SEO structure from the start to help businesses grow online.
