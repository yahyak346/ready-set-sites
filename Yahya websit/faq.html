Frequently Asked Questions

Ready Set Site is a web design studio that builds clean, modern and affordable websites for small businesses, trades and startups across the UK. From one-page starter sites to full business websites and SEO, we make getting online simple, fast and stress-free.

Got a question before we start? Here are the answers we are asked most often.

How much does a website cost?
Our websites start from £100 for a one-page starter site. Full multi-page business websites and SEO are priced on exactly what you need, so you never pay for extras that do not help your business. The best way to get an accurate figure is to ask for a free, no-obligation quote and we will talk you through your options in plain English.
How long does it take to build my website?
Most one-page starter websites go live within a few days. Larger business websites usually take one to two weeks depending on the number of pages, the content involved and how quickly you can send us your details. Whatever the size of the project, we give you a clear timeline before we begin so you always know what to expect.
Do I own my website?
Yes, completely. Once your website is finished and paid for, it is 100% yours to keep, along with your domain name. You are never locked in, and if you ever choose to move it elsewhere, everything belongs to you.
What about hosting and a domain name?
We help you set up reliable, fast hosting and register your domain name (your web address, such as yourbusiness.co.uk). We guide you through the whole process step by step, so even if you have never done this before, you will never feel stuck or confused.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
Absolutely. More than half of all web visitors are on a phone, so every website we build is fully mobile-friendly. Your site will automatically adjust to look sharp and load quickly on phones, tablets, laptops and desktops.
Do you help with Google and SEO?
Yes. Basic SEO setup is included with every website, which means proper page titles, descriptions, clean structure and a sitemap so Google can find and understand your site. If you want to rank higher for competitive local searches, our dedicated SEO service goes further with keyword research and Google Business Profile setup.
Can you update or redesign my website later?
Of course. Your business will grow and change, and your website can grow with it. We offer updates, new pages, seasonal changes and full redesigns whenever you need them — just get in touch and we will take care of it.
What do you need from me to get started?
Very little to begin with: your business name, a short description of what you do, the pages you would like and any websites whose style you like. Do not worry if you do not have your text or photos ready yet — we can help with all of that.
Do you write the text and provide images?
Yes. We can write clear, professional copy for your pages and source high-quality, licensed images so you do not have to. If you already have your own logo, photographs or wording, we will happily use them to keep everything on-brand.
Do you offer payment in instalments?
For larger projects we can usually split the cost into simple, manageable instalments. Just mention it when you enquire and we will find a payment option that comfortably suits your budget.
Which areas do you cover?
We work with businesses right across the UK. Everything is handled online and over the phone or WhatsApp, so wherever you are based, your location is never a problem.
Why should I choose Ready Set Site?
We focus on clean, modern designs that are genuinely affordable, with honest pricing and no confusing jargon. You deal with us directly, get fast turnarounds and end up with a website that is built to do one thing above all — turn visitors into paying customers.

Still have a question?

Message us on WhatsApp or send an enquiry and we will get right back to you.
