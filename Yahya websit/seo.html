What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation. In plain English, it is the work that helps your website show up on Google when people search for what you offer. When someone types "plumber near me" or "best cafe in town", SEO decides whether your business appears near the top - or gets buried on page five where nobody looks.

It includes using the right words on your pages, writing clear titles and descriptions, and setting up your Google Business Profile. Good SEO means more of the right people find you, without paying for every single click.
