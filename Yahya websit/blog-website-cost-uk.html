How Much Does a Small Business Website Cost in the UK?

Website prices vary depending on the number of pages, features and design requirements.

Many freelancers charge between £100 and £1000 for small business websites, while larger agencies often charge several thousand pounds.

The main factors affecting cost include:

  • Number of pages
  • Custom design
  • SEO setup
  • Contact forms
  • Blog functionality
  • Google integrations

For most small businesses, a professional website does not need to cost thousands of pounds to be effective.
