What is a business website?

A business website is your company's home on the internet - a set of connected pages (homepage, services, about and contact) that tell people who you are, what you offer and how to reach you. Unlike a single page, it gives each part of your business room to breathe so customers find exactly what they need.

It also builds trust. When someone searches for your business and lands on a clean, professional website, they feel confident getting in touch - which means more enquiries and more sales.
